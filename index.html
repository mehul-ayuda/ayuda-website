FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program for?
Working professionals looking to transition into Product Management. Engineers, designers, marketers, business analysts, sales professionals. If you're serious about PM, this is for you.
What does "unlimited 1:1s" actually mean?
Schedule as many one-on-one sessions with Malay as you need. Interview prep, career doubts, portfolio review, product thinking. No cap. Not TAs. Malay himself.
I'm already a PM. Is this relevant for me?
Absolutely. We've had PMs level up to Senior PM or transition to better companies. Alumni have moved to Microsoft, Amazon, boAt, TikTok, and more.
How is Ayuda different from other PM programs?
Three things: (1) Malay is a practicing Director of Product, not a full-time educator. (2) Unlimited personal 1:1s with him, not TAs. (3) Curriculum built around practical proof-of-work, not just theoretical frameworks.
What's the time commitment per week?
Around 8-10 hours per week including live sessions, assignments, 1:1s, and self-study. Sessions are scheduled outside standard work hours.
